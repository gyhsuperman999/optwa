Thank you for using Text‑to‑Speech Pro ("we", "our", or the "Service"). This page explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our tools for text‑to‑speech conversion, cross‑language dubbing, and related features.

1. Information We Collect

  • Content you provide: text or audio you submit, selected target languages and voices, conversion parameters, and any feedback or contact details (e.g., email) you share with us.
  • Usage logs & device data: request timestamps, IP address (for abuse prevention and regional compliance), browser/device type, page/API call logs, error logs, and performance metrics.
  • Cookies/local storage: used for session continuity, remembering preferences, authentication, and abuse prevention (e.g., rate‑limiting or CAPTCHA). You can manage or clear cookies in your browser.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve core features such as voice conversion and synthesis;
  • For diagnostics, analytics, and abuse prevention (e.g., detecting anomalous or automated traffic);
  • To communicate product updates, important notices, and support responses;
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

3. Third‑Party Processing & International Transfers

To perform speech recognition/transcription, translation, and synthesis, we may transmit necessary data (such as the text or short audio segments you submit) over encrypted channels to one or more cloud or model providers for processing. Providers may change over time for reasons of performance, cost, and compliance. We strive to work with reputable vendors and protect your data via encryption and data‑minimization.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Generated audio/text is used to deliver results and short‑term quality assurance by default and is not retained long‑term unless needed for billing/reconciliation, legal obligations, or you explicitly choose to save it.
  • Log data is kept for the minimum necessary period (generally no longer than 90 days) for security auditing and troubleshooting, after which it is deleted or de‑identified.
  • You may request deletion of your data via the contact method below; we will process requests within a reasonable time.

5. Sharing & Disclosure

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may disclose information only with your explicit consent; as necessary to perform the Service (hosting, compute, storage, CDN, security); or as required by law, law enforcement, or to protect the safety of the Service and its users.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, correction, and deletion of your information;
  • Withdraw consent for specific processing (without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal);
  • Obtain a copy of your data where technically feasible.

7. Children & Sensitive Content

The Service is intended for users aged 16 and above. Please do not upload content containing minors’ personal information, medical/financial/government ID data, voiceprints/biometrics, or other sensitive personal data. Ensure you hold the necessary legal rights to the text/audio you submit; do not use the Service for impersonation, deception, or unlawful purposes.

8. Security

We use measures such as HTTPS, access controls, least‑privilege practices, and audit logging to protect data. However, no online service can be 100% secure; please safeguard your account and generated content appropriately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect feature changes or legal requirements. We will update the “Last updated” date above and, for material changes, provide an in‑app notice or email (where applicable).
